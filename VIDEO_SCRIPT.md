## Technical Production Notes

### Video Specifications
- **Duration**: Exactly 60 seconds
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Audio**: Clear voiceover with background music (subtle, professional)

### Screen Recording Requirements
- Clean browser window (no unnecessary tabs)
- Cursor highlighting enabled
- Smooth transitions between scenes
- Clear, legible text (minimum 16pt font)
- High contrast for readability

### Animation Guidelines
- Smooth fade transitions (0.3s duration)
- Professional color scheme (blues, grays, accent green for success)
- Consistent motion graphics
- No jarring cuts or rapid changes
- Accessibility-friendly (avoid flashing lights)

### Audio Specifications
- Voiceover: Professional, clear, moderate pace
- Background music: Subtle, tech-focused, non-distracting
- Volume balance: Voice primary, music at 20% volume
- No audio peaks or distortion

### On-Screen Text Standards
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Helvetica, Arial, or Inter)
- Title text: 48pt bold
- Body text: 24pt regular
- Code/addresses: 18pt monospace
- High contrast (white text on dark background or vice versa)

### Branding Elements
- Digital Court logo: Gavel + blockchain icon
- Color palette:
  - Primary: #2C3E50 (dark blue-gray)
  - Accent: #3498DB (bright blue)
  - Success: #27AE60 (green)
  - Warning: #F39C12 (orange)
  - Text: #ECF0F1 (light gray) on dark backgrounds

### Accessibility Considerations
- Closed captions available (exported separately)
- Clear visual indicators for all actions
- High contrast ratios (WCAG AA compliant)
- No reliance on color alone for information
- Readable font sizes throughout

### Platform-Specific Optimization
- YouTube: Include timestamp chapters in description
- Twitter/X: Create 30-second teaser version
- LinkedIn: Professional tone, emphasize enterprise use cases
- GitHub: Embed directly in README with fallback link
